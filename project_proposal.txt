Project Title: Fitness Training

Team: Freddy, Shriya, Sanmi, Kassidy

Project Description: We aim to investigate the relationship between gym accessibility factors (cost, location, transportation, and income level) and individuals' training preferences (at-home, gym, endurance marathon). 

Research Question(s): How does accessibility to the gym affect training preferences? accessibility = [cost, location, transportation, income level] 
training preferences = [at-home, gym, endurance marathon]

Datasets: Kraggle (fitness search term tbd), gym websites, census api for income, transportation, training costs, 

Tasks: Question Making, Data Finding, Data Cleaning, Data Analyzing, Data Modeling, Writing
